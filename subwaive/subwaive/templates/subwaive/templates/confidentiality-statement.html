Confidential Information

We never release information except:

  • About a member or membership to that member
  • About a membership to the person who pays for it
  • The specifically requested information with a valid court order

This includes:

  • If somebody is or has ever been a member
  • When or whether somebody visited
  • Purchase histories
  • Anything related to signed documents except:
    • Documents required of board members
    • Whether an individual has a current volunteer agreement
